 Chocolate Sales Analysis: From Dashboard to Business Insight

📊 Project Overview

This project goes beyond building a dashboard — it focuses on turning data into business insights.

Using a chocolate sales dataset, I explored performance differences across countries and identified why Canada underperforms compared to Australia

---

 Business Question

<<<  Why is Canada underperforming compared to Australia?  >>

---

 Hypothesis

The issue is not the market itself, but differences in product category performance

---

🧪 Data Validation

* Flavored products perform **~1.9x better in Australia**
* Share of flavored products in total sales:

  * 🇨🇦 Canada → **15.6%**
  * 🇦🇺 Australia → **25.1%**

---

🔍 Key Findings

* Flavored/sweet products underperform in Canada
* Classic & nut-based products perform strongly
* Top-selling products in Canada are mostly classic categories

---

Insight

👉 Canada is not a weak market
👉 It reflects different customer preferences

---

💼 Business Recommendations

* Focus on classic & nut-based products in Canada
* Reposition flavored products:

  * Pricing
  * Messaging
  * Packaging
* Avoid a one-size-fits-all global strategy

---

 ⚠️ Limitations

* Based on Kaggle dataset
* No access to:

  * Customer behavior
  * Pricing strategies
  * External market factors

➡️ Results should be considered directional, not definitive
